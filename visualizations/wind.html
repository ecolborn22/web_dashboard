Wind Speed

This visualization helps to illustrate the relationship between wind speed and latitude of various cities across the world. Wind speed has been plotted as a function of the latitude of the respective city. From the plot, it can be noted that high positive latitudes posess some data points with significantly higher wind speeds than their southern counterparts. However, most of the points fall between 0 and 20 mph. There is not a strong observable correlation between these two variables.
